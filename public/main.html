This project aims to display in a visual way how musicians of the past were connected to each other.

  • Use the mouse wheel to zoom in & out.
  • Click in an empty spot and hold to drag the graph around.
  • Click on a box to display information about a person.
  • Click on a box and hold to drag it horizontally.

The meaning of the colors are displayed on the right table. Unconfirmed means there is no proof about said relationship or the sources are doubtful. For example, Francesco Geminiani continues to be regarded as a student of Lonati despite lack of proof.

To know more about the project, visit its project page.
