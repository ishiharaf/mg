Basic operation:

  • Use the mouse wheel to zoom in & out.
  • Click in an empty spot and hold to drag the graph around.
  • Click on a box and hold to drag it horizontally.
  • Click on a box to display information about a person.
  • Click in an empty spot to clear the selection.
  • Click on the gear icon to change the graph options.
  • Click on the loupe icon to search for someone.

The meaning of the colors are displayed on the left. Unconfirmed means there is no proof about said relationship or the sources are doubtful. e.g.: Francesco Geminiani continues to be regarded as a student of Lonati despite lack of proof.
